Demo to visitors from Northwestern Polytechnical University

[top]

[top]

Prof. Chen Jianyou, Prof. Yanning Zhang, Prof. Zhou Deyun, Prof. Zhan Hao and Prof. Pan Quan from Northwestern Polytechnical University visited our 3D Motion Capture Laboratory. Dr. Howard Leung asked his Ph.D. students to present various research work. Liu Zhiguang first introduced the 3D Motion Capture Technology. Jacky Chan gave a live demonstration of the Dance Education System. Zhou Liuyang presented the human motion retreival demo using Kinect. Yang Lingling presented the research on brain computer interface.
